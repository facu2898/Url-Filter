Instructions

To use this filter first run the product package with your freshely collected URLs. Then upload that product package export and your URLs in xlsx format in the upload boxes on your left.(DO NOT USE OTHER FORMATS). Finally choose the tolerance. This is the number of unique products, over one hundred products, that the filter will tolerate before keeping the URL. For example, with a tolerance of 1, in a URL of 800 items, the filter will tolerate 8 unique items before NOT discarding the URL. Finally press upload and wait. An excel file should be downloaded with the filtered URLs.
